ABOUT US

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, established in 2018 is a student body aimed at developing a satrt-up culture in the G.B. Pant University of Agriculture and Technology,Pantnagar. It focuses on spreading awareness about entrepreneurship among students and helping them in establishing their own start-up journey by providing them a proper platorm to interact with incubators, seeding funds and investors. It also aims at increasing job opportunities and placements in the university and grooming skills of students throughout the university, from the very first year. This plan will be achieved by organizing various workshops, mentorship programmes and competitions thus helping them develop and hone their skills and knowledge required for establishing start-ups and/or securing placements.

FOOTPRINTS' 24

The Annual Business Fest

Footprints, the mega event of Entrepreneurship Development Cell involves a series of events, focused on promoting entrepreneurship & innovation. It aims to foster networking opportunities, provide valuable insights from industry experts, and an opportunity for students to present the startups and promote collaboration between academia and the business world. Rigorous panel discussions, pitch competitions, career fairs, and networking sessions are organised to engage students, alumni, faculty, and industry professionals. Footprints serves as a platform to inspire, educate, and connect students interested in the business world.

INNOVATION CONCLAVE

Participants prepare a brief presentation designed to showcase your business plan, eye-popping metrices and vision for the company to raise capital from investors. It encourages innovation within the campus but also fosters collaboration between academia and entrepreneurial world.

THE ART EXPO

Event that combines art with business and marketing. Participants will showcase their paintings and sketches or any other art form in the exhibition.

BUSINESS FAIR

In this event entrepreneurs from different firms will come and showcase their products in the stall provided by E-Cell. A fair where all the faculties from different college of Pantnagar will come and explore the fair.

NOSTALGIA NEXUS

In this event alumni of E-Cell from batch 2018 to 2023 will be invited to come together and reconnect with their peers, and faculty members and also serve as a platform for the institute to engage with their graduates and gain insights into their career progress and achievements.

PANORAMIC PANTNAGAR

It will be an online event in which students have to make a short video on Pantnagar with their point of view and it will be posted by marketing team of E-Cell on social media pages of E-Cell and the best 3 videos will be awarded in footprints.

CONTEN CREATION

This event is going to invite an influencer with a strong online following on social media platforms to share and inspire the journey with students.

GALLERY
