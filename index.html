ABOUT US

E-Cell or the Entrepreneurship-Cell is a student body aimed at developing a start-up culture in the G.B. Pant University of Agriculture and Technology,Pantnagar. It focuses on spreading awareness about entrepreneurship among students and helping them in establishing their own start-up journey by providing them a proper platform to interact with incubators, seeding funds and investors. It also aims at increasing job opportunities andplacements in the university and grooming skills of students throughout the university, from the very first year. This plan will be achieved by organizing various workshops, mentorship programmes and competitions thus helping them develop and hone their skills and knowledge required for establishing start-ups and or securing placements.
