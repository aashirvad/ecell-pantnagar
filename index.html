ABOUT US

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, established in 2018 is a student body aimed at developing a satrt-up culture in the G.B. Pant University of Agriculture and Technology,Pantnagar. It focuses on spreading awareness about entrepreneurship among students and helping them in establishing their own start-up journey by providing them a proper platorm to interact with incubators, seeding funds and investors. It also aims at increasing job opportunities and placements in the university and grooming skills of students throughout the university, from the very first year. This plan will be achieved by organizing various workshops, mentorship programmes and competitions thus helping them develop and hone their skills and knowledge required for establishing start-ups and/or securing placements.
